# local-lift
# Strengthening Local Economic Collaboration through Digital Connectivity

> A digital platform designed to connect local product creators, retailers, and service providers to promote inclusive economic growth, increase business opportunities, and strengthen community collaboration.

## 🌍 SDG Alignment

This project directly supports **United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth** by enabling small businesses, local entrepreneurs, and independent service providers to access wider markets and build sustainable livelihoods.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Local economies often face a lack of coordination between:

* Product creators and artisans
* Retailers and local businesses
* Service providers and customers

Many talented makers and small entrepreneurs struggle to gain visibility, while retailers find it difficult to discover unique, locally produced goods and reliable services.

This disconnect leads to:

* Limited business opportunities
* Reduced income generation
* Inefficient supply chains
* Underutilized local talent
* Slower economic growth within communities

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

**Strengthening Local Economic Collaboration through Digital Connectivity** is a digital platform that bridges the gap between local stakeholders by creating a centralized ecosystem where creators, retailers, and service providers can discover, connect, collaborate, and grow together.

The platform aims to foster a stronger local economy by enabling:

* Product discovery
* Business networking
* Direct communication
* Collaboration opportunities
* Market expansion

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Increase visibility for local entrepreneurs and artisans
* Simplify product and service discovery for retailers
* Encourage collaboration among local businesses
* Create new income-generation opportunities
* Support sustainable and inclusive economic growth
* Strengthen local supply chains through digital connectivity

## 🔄 How It Works

1. Users create an account and complete their business profile.
2. Creators list their products or services.
3. Retailers search for relevant local offerings.
4. Users communicate through the platform.
5. Partnerships and transactions are established.
6. Businesses grow through continuous collaboration.
